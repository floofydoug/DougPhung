Bio

The Floofy name comes from a group of friends in college who also had Floofy names. There was a FloofySeal, a FloofyDugong, a FloofyOtter, but I was FloofyDoug. I ended up using the name for everything, from my Dota2 account to my Twitter, and my blog... it's a name I'm quite proud of.

What I Do

I offer free web consulting for small-businesses looking to build or re-build their site. Set up a meeting with me, and I will give you my opinions and whether we start the real work is up to you. I've dabbled with many different web technologies and will try to pinpoint a solution that will best fit your business. Then, depending on the project, I will start developing right away, or we can start collaborating on some mock-ups or wire-frames using Balsamiq. I record all my time using Toggl , so you can view nice little reports about where my time is being spent. Here are some buzz-words about the platforms and frameworks I work with: Twitter Bootstrap, PureCSS, HTML5, CSS3, WordPress, Drupal, and Ruby on Rails.
