The Usual Bio

The Floofy name comes from a group of friends in college who also had Floofy names. There was a FloofySeal, a FloofyDugong, a FloofyOtter, but I was FloofyDoug. I ended up using the name for everything, from my gamertag to my Twitter and blog... it's a name I'm quite proud of.

I offer free web consulting for small-businesses looking to build or re-build their sites. Set up a meeting with me, and I will give you my honest opinions, whether we start the real work is up to you. I've dabbled with many different web technologies and will try to pinpoint a solution that will best fit your business. Here are some buzz-words about the platforms and frameworks I work with: Twitter Bootstrap, PureCSS, HTML5, CSS3, WordPress, Drupal, and Ruby on Rails.

If you like what you see, let me know and let's start collaborating.
